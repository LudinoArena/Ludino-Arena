Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6th March 2026

Ludino Arena ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile game that respects your privacy. This policy describes what information we collect, if any, and how we use it.

1. Information we collect

Ludino Arena is designed to work with minimal data collection:

  • Local play (Pass & Play, AI mode): We do not collect any personal information. Game progress and settings may be stored only on your device.
  • Device data: We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or similar data for tracking purposes.

2. How we use information

Any data that remains on your device (e.g. game preferences) is used only to improve your experience within the app. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-party services

The app may use third-party services (e.g. for analytics or crash reporting). If we do, we will use providers that comply with applicable privacy laws and will describe them here or in the app. Currently, Ludino Arena does not integrate analytics or advertising SDKs that collect personal data.

4. Data storage and security

Data stored on your device is subject to your device’s security and your choices (e.g. app permissions). We do not transmit or store your personal data on our servers for local and AI gameplay.

5. Children’s privacy

Ludino Arena is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page and, where appropriate, notify you in the app. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
